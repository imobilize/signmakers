Metal Workers Systems Limited

Metal Workers is the new name for Thames Wire Productions, which was formerly known as Thames Wire Productions.

Established in 1963, Thames Wire Productions has a rich history of crafting top-tier metal fabrications for both the film and construction industries. Our expertise spans various materials, including stainless steel, aluminum, brass, as well as the commonly used mild steel. Over the years, we have proudly manufactured balustrades for renowned global retailers and multinational corporate headquarters. Our commitment to precision and quality is reflected in our fully equipped facilities, which include in-house tube rolling and bending, sheet forming and rolling, and a comprehensive machine shop equipped for turning and milling. We collaborate closely with clients to bring their designs to life, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of craftsmanship.
