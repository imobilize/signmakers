Specialists in Sign Board Designing and Making

At Sign Makers, we pride ourselves as the Specialists in Sign Board Designing and Making. With a wealth of experience and a dedicated team of experts, we bring a deep understanding of the art and science behind creating impactful signage. Our commitment to excellence extends from the initial concept and design phase to the meticulous craftsmanship in our manufacturing process. Whether you require eye-catching storefront signs, informative directional boards, or any other signage solution, our specialized approach ensures that your unique vision is brought to life with precision and flair

Sign Makers Limited

Sign Makers, formerly known as Parkinson Signs, carries a rich legacy dating back to its establishment in 1946. As a family-owned and operated business, we have consistently upheld a tradition of excellence in the world of signage. Our expertise spans the entire spectrum of sign-related services, encompassing top-tier design, precise manufacturing, seamless installation, and meticulous maintenance.

With a strong presence across the UK, we cater to a diverse clientele, ranging from single, one-off contracts to comprehensive nationwide installations for both national accounts and individual projects. Our commitment to quality extends beyond installation, as we also offer maintenance contracts to ensure that all types of signs remain in optimal condition. At Sign Makers, we take pride in our decades-long experience and unwavering dedication to delivering outstanding sign solutions that stand the test of time.
